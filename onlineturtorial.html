How Our Online Classes Work

1

Register for a Class

Choose from our variety of dance styles and time slots that work best for you. Complete your registration and payment online.

2

Receive Zoom Link

After registration, you'll receive a confirmation email with the Zoom meeting link and password. Save this information for class time.

3

Set Up Your Space

Clear a small area for movement, ensure your device is charged, and test your audio/video settings before class begins.

4

Join The Live Class

Click the Zoom link 5-10 minutes before class starts. Our instructor will welcome you and guide you through the session.

5

Dance & Enjoy

Follow along with the live instruction, ask questions, and receive real-time feedback just like in a physical studio.
